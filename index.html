About Cameron

I am a software engineer from beautiful SoCal. A native Californian and Eagle Scout, you can find me hiking, biking, camping, and scuba diving. Sometimes I take photos along the way.

I follow developments but Simulations, AI, Artificial Limbs, Autonomous Vehicles, and remote sensor technology. My daily work supports NAVAIR software, but during off days, I explore the sunny California coast and take on projects.

I am passionate about environmental preservation and would love to someday tie that into my work. If you need a hand or want to explore with me, get in touch!
